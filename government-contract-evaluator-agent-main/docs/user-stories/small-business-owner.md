# User Story: Small Business Owner

## Persona: Sarah Chen

**Role**: Owner of a small IT consulting firm
**Company Size**: 15 employees
**Experience**: 8 years in business, new to government contracting
**Certifications**: 8(a), WOSB
**Goal**: Win first government contract

---

## User Journey

### 1. Discovery and Registration

**Scenario**: Sarah heard about government contracting opportunities and wants to explore if her company qualifies.

**Actions**:
1. Visits GovAI website
2. Reviews features and pricing
3. Clicks "Get Started" to register
4. Creates account with email and password
5. Verifies email address
6. Logs in to dashboard

**Acceptance Criteria**:
- Registration completes in under 2 minutes
- Email verification works seamlessly
- Clear onboarding path after login

---

### 2. Company Onboarding

**Scenario**: Sarah needs to set up her company profile to receive relevant opportunities.

**Actions**:
1. Starts 3-step onboarding wizard
2. **Step 1 - Company Info**:
   - Enters company name: "Chen IT Solutions"
   - Selects legal structure: LLC
   - Enters UEI number
   - Provides business address
3. **Step 2 - Capabilities**:
   - Searches and selects NAICS codes:
     - 541511 (Custom Computer Programming)
     - 541512 (Computer Systems Design)
     - 541519 (Other Computer Related Services)
   - Selects certifications: 8(a), WOSB
   - Sets contract range: $50,000 - $2,000,000
   - Selects geographic preferences: VA, MD, DC
4. **Step 3 - Capabilities Statement**:
   - Writes description of company capabilities
   - Highlights past performance and expertise
   - Submits onboarding

**Acceptance Criteria**:
- NAICS code search is intuitive
- Can select up to 10 NAICS codes
- Certifications are clearly explained
- Progress saved between steps
- Onboarding completes in under 10 minutes

---

### 3. Reviewing AI-Evaluated Opportunities

**Scenario**: Sarah wants to find suitable contract opportunities for her company.

**Actions**:
1. Navigates to Opportunities page
2. Reviews list of AI-evaluated opportunities
3. Sees recommendations: BID (green), RESEARCH (yellow), NO_BID (red)
4. Filters by:
   - Recommendation: BID only
   - Minimum fit score: 70%
5. Clicks on high-scoring opportunity
6. Reviews detailed analysis:
   - Fit score: 85/100
   - Win probability: 68%
   - Strengths identified
   - Weaknesses noted
   - Key requirements listed
   - Risk factors explained

**Acceptance Criteria**:
- Opportunities load within 3 seconds
- Filtering works intuitively
- AI recommendations are clearly displayed
- Detailed analysis provides actionable insights
- Response deadline is prominently shown

---

### 4. Managing the Pipeline

**Scenario**: Sarah wants to track opportunities she's interested in.

**Actions**:
1. Saves promising opportunity as "WATCHING"
2. Adds personal note: "Review with team on Monday"
3. Moves opportunity to "BIDDING" status
4. Views pipeline board showing all saved opportunities
5. Updates notes as proposal progresses
6. Marks opportunity as "WON" after contract award

**Acceptance Criteria**:
- One-click saving to pipeline
- Notes field supports sufficient text
- Pipeline board shows clear status columns
- Can drag/move opportunities between statuses
- Win rate tracking updates automatically

---

### 5. Email Notifications

**Scenario**: Sarah wants to stay informed about new opportunities without constantly checking the platform.

**Actions**:
1. Goes to Settings page
2. Configures email frequency: Daily digest
3. Receives daily email at 8 AM with:
   - New BID recommendations
   - Opportunities matching her profile
   - Deadline reminders
4. Clicks email link to view opportunity details
5. Receives reminder 3 days before deadline

**Acceptance Criteria**:
- Email settings are easy to configure
- Daily digest arrives consistently
- Email contains relevant information
- Links work correctly
- Deadline reminders are timely

---

## User Needs

### Must Have
- Simple registration and onboarding
- Accurate NAICS code matching
- Clear BID/NO_BID recommendations
- Deadline tracking
- Mobile-friendly interface

### Should Have
- Detailed AI analysis explaining recommendations
- Pipeline management
- Email notifications
- Search and filter capabilities

### Nice to Have
- Team collaboration features
- Proposal templates
- Historical win/loss analysis
- Integration with other tools

---

## Success Metrics

| Metric | Target |
|--------|--------|
| Time to complete onboarding | < 10 minutes |
| Relevant opportunities per week | 10+ |
| Time saved vs manual search | 5+ hours/week |
| Opportunities moved to pipeline | 3+/month |
| User satisfaction score | 4.5/5 |

---

## Pain Points Addressed

1. **Information Overload**: AI filters thousands of opportunities to show only relevant ones
2. **Missed Deadlines**: Automated reminders ensure timely submissions
3. **Capability Matching**: AI evaluates fit based on company profile
4. **Decision Fatigue**: Clear BID/NO_BID recommendations
5. **Tracking Chaos**: Centralized pipeline management
